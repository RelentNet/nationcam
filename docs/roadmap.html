Add-ons (post-core)

IMA · Programmatic ads — Google Ad Manager + IMA SDK as fill behind direct-sold local ads (EarthCam's exact stack). Needs GAM approval + consent management; adopt once traffic justifies it.

ADP · Advertiser portal — self-serve campaigns on top of the P3 schema + P4 roles: advertisers manage their own creatives, targeting, and see per-camera impression/click reports.

Critical path from here

P1 SSR relaunch → P2 Camera pages → P3 Local ads → P4 Accounts/RBAC → P5 Camera model → P6 Restream

P7 Viewer UX layers onto P2 incrementally (weather → map → favorites) and doesn't block the path. Revenue arrives at P3; the differentiator vs EarthCam lands at P6.

Deferred — long-term ideas

  • TV — NationCamTV — lean-back ambient channel (rotating best cams, TV apps)
  • B2B — Construction / time-lapse monitoring SaaS (EarthCam.net's revenue model)
  • EMB — Embeddable player for third-party sites (traffic + backlink flywheel)

EarthCam legs worth copying eventually, parked until the core platform ships.
